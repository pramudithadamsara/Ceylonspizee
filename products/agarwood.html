Ceylon Agarwood

Premium Oud Chips & Essential Oil

Experience the mystical aroma of Ceylon Agarwood — a luxurious resinous wood harvested from the ancient Aquilaria trees in Sri Lanka. Our agarwood is sustainably sourced and meticulously hand-processed to ensure high resin content and rich aroma. Ideal for incense ceremonies, aromatherapy, and premium perfumery applications.

How to Use Ceylon Agarwood

Burning as Incense

Place agarwood chips over hot charcoal or an electric burner. The slow smoldering releases a deep, meditative fragrance ideal for spiritual practices and home sanctification.

Perfumery & Blending

Agarwood oil is a prized base note in niche perfumery. Blend with floral or citrus top notes to create long-lasting, exotic scents that captivate the senses.

Spiritual & Wellness Use

Use agarwood in rituals to purify space, calm the mind, and connect with higher consciousness. Ideal for yoga, meditation, and stress relief practices.

Add the sacred fragrance of Ceylon Agarwood to your wellness rituals and luxury scent creations.
