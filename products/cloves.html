Ceylon Cloves

Whole Organic Clove Buds

Our Ceylon Cloves are hand-picked flower buds harvested at peak maturity and sun-dried to preserve natural essential oils. With their warm, sweet-spicy scent and potent eugenol levels, these cloves are perfect for gourmet recipes, herbal infusions, and natural wellness blends.

Key Product Highlights

  • Origin: Gampola, Sri Lanka (Single-farm source)
  • Processing: Hand-harvested, sun-dried
  • Main Compound: Eugenol (80–90%)
  • Applications: Baking, chai spice, pickling, oral care, herbal remedies

Organic & Naturally Processed

Our cloves are certified organic, non-GMO, and Fair Trade. Each batch is cleaned and sorted without additives or artificial drying.

How to Use Ceylon Cloves

Culinary

Add to curries, desserts, and spice blends. Whole or ground cloves impart warm, rich flavor.

Natural Remedies

Use in herbal teas, steam inhalation, or DIY mouthwash. Eugenol is known for antimicrobial properties.

Storage

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place away from light and heat sources.
