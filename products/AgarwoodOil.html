Ceylon Agarwood Oil

Steam-Distilled Sri Lankan Oud Oil

Ceylon Agarwood Oil, also known as Sri Lankan Oud Oil, is a rare and exquisite essential oil extracted from premium-grade agarwood. Steam-distilled in small batches, it delivers a bold, smoky, woody aroma with sweet resinous undertones. A treasured ingredient for niche perfumery, spiritual incense blends, and deep relaxation.

Key Features

  • Extraction: Hydro-distillation (steam process)
  • Origin: Ethically sourced from Southern Sri Lanka
  • Fragrance Notes: Woody, resinous, leathery, sweet, smoky
  • Use Cases: Luxury perfumes, incense blends, meditation, anointing oil

Ethical & Authentic

Our oud oil is sourced from CITES-regulated plantations. We ensure no chemical solvents are used and maintain eco-conscious distillation methods that preserve purity and potency.

How to Use Oud Oil

Perfumery

Apply a drop behind the ears or use as a base note in artisan perfume blends. Its deep, resinous scent creates a long-lasting impression.

Spiritual & Ritual

Use during meditation, prayer, or anointing for grounding, calming, and spiritual purification.

Diffuser or Warmer

Add a drop to an oil warmer or diffuser for natural room fragrance that enhances tranquility and clarity.
