Ceylon Cinnamon Oil

Steam-Distilled Cinnamon Leaf Oil

Extracted from hand-harvested Ceylon Cinnamon leaves, our Cinnamon Oil is steam-distilled in small batches to preserve its therapeutic properties. With a warm, spicy aroma and high eugenol content, it is ideal for aromatherapy, natural perfumery, skincare, and cleaning solutions.

Key Product Highlights

  • Origin: Kegalle, Sri Lanka (Estate-grown)
  • Extraction: Steam Distilled from Cinnamon Leaves
  • Main Component: Eugenol (70–85%)
  • Applications: Aromatherapy, massage oils, cosmetics, home cleaning, insect repellent

Certified & Sustainably Sourced

Our Cinnamon Oil is 100% pure, USDA organic certified, and free from additives or solvents. Produced under GMP conditions with ethical sourcing practices.
