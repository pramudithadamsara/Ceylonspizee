Ceylon Cardamom

Sweet & Aromatic Green Cardamom

Grown in Sri Lanka’s cool central highlands, our Ceylon Cardamom offers an intense burst of citrusy-sweet aroma and flavor. Handpicked and naturally dried, these pods are perfect for chai blends, desserts, and traditional Ayurvedic preparations.

Key Product Highlights

  • Origin: Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka (High-altitude)
  • Processing: Handpicked, sun-dried, sorted
  • Flavor: Citrusy, sweet, refreshing
  • Applications: Tea, desserts, biryani, traditional medicine

Certified & Sustainable

Organically grown and processed without chemicals. Our cardamom is certified organic and fair trade compliant.

How to Use Ceylon Cardamom

In Cooking

Crush or use whole in curries, biryanis, and spice mixes for a sweet herbal kick.

In Teas

Add pods to black or herbal tea for a refreshing and aromatic twist.

Storage

Keep in sealed pouches away from sunlight and moisture to preserve oils and flavor.
